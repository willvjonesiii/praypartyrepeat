The Journey

Walking Through Each Element

⬡
Month One

The Element of Earth

We begin where all healing must begin — in the body. Earth is the foundation upon which every other transformation rests. This month we ground, root, and become fully present in our physical vessel. We face what we've avoided, create the stable container we need, and lay the altar for the months ahead.

Through somatic practices, ancestral connection, and deep grounding ceremonies, you will begin to trust your body as sacred ground — perhaps for the first time.

Focus: Grounding · Embodiment · Ancestral Healing
〰
Month Two

The Element of Water

Water does not fight its path — it finds it. This month we surrender to the current of what we've long held beneath the surface. Grief, longing, unexpressed emotion, old wounds that never had a container. Water teaches us that release is not weakness — it is how we become free.

Through cord cutting ceremonies, emotional release practices, and sound healing, you will learn to flow with your emotional intelligence rather than be swept away by it.

Focus: Emotional Release · Flow · Cord Cutting
△
Month Three

The Element of Fire

Fire is the element of transformation, courage, and sacred destruction. At the center of this journey stands the fire that burns away what no longer serves. Old identities, limiting stories, patterns inherited from generations before us — they meet their end here.

Through transformational hypnosis, fire ceremonies, and Akashic Record healing, you will step into your full power — not around the fire, but through it.

Focus: Transformation · Courage · Subconscious Rewiring
○
Month Four

The Element of Air

With the weight of what was released, Air arrives. The mind clears. Breath, which was shallow and guarded, begins to deepen. You begin to see your life — your relationships, your purpose, your possibilities — from an entirely new elevation.

Breathwork, vision practices, and mental clarity sessions help you articulate the new narrative of your life and create the roadmap for living it fully.

Focus: Breathwork · Mental Clarity · Vision
✦
Month Five

The Element of Spirit

The fifth element weaves all others into one unified field of awareness. Spirit is not somewhere else — it is the thread running through everything you've walked through. This month, you integrate, celebrate, and claim your new self with ceremony and community.

Through a closing ceremony, a final 1:1 with Marinés, and a ritual of gratitude, you complete this rite of passage knowing exactly who you are and why you are here.

Focus: Integration · Purpose · Sacred Completion

Questions

Frequently Asked

What makes this different from a regular healing course or workshop? +

Most courses give you information. This program gives you experience. Over five months you will be held, challenged, witnessed, and guided through an actual transformation — not just educated about one. The combination of private 1:1 sessions, live group ceremony, and community creates a container that single workshops simply cannot replicate.

I've tried programs before and they didn't hold. How is this different? +

The reason most programs don't hold is that they stop at information or one-time experience without sustained integration. This program is built around integration as its core principle. Five months means the work compounds. Each element builds on the last. You don't just have an experience — you become it.

How much time do I need to commit each month? +

At minimum: one 90-minute group ceremony call and one 60-minute private session per month. Additionally, you're invited (never required) to engage with the monthly ritual guide and community space. Most participants find themselves naturally drawn to spend more time with the material — which is a sign the healing is working.

Is this fully virtual? Can I join from anywhere in the world? +

Yes. Walking with the Elements is fully virtual and has welcomed participants from across the country and internationally. All sessions are held via video call. Time zones are considered when scheduling group calls, and recordings are always available for those who cannot attend live.

What if I miss a live ceremony call? +

Life happens — and it is honored here. All group ceremony calls are recorded and shared with participants within 24 hours. You will never lose access to the content. However, we do encourage live attendance when possible, as the live container carries something that recordings cannot fully replicate.

How do I know if I'm ready for this level of work? +

If you've been drawn to this page and read this far, some part of you already knows. Readiness is not about being healed enough — it's about being honest enough to begin. The discovery call with Marinés is the right place to explore this question together. There is no pressure, only discernment.
